Our Services

We are here to work with your vision, not against your budget. We’re creative as hell with what we can do within limits. Respect our work, and we’ll overdeliver.

Branding

  • Single Logo: One high-resolution, custom logo + one revision
  • Logo Variants: Two high-resolution, custom logos + one revision each
  • Branding Pack: Two logos + three proofs of use + font pair + three color palette

Design

  • Marketing Materials: One flyer, social media post, etc.
  • Detailed Graphics: One custom album cover, book illustration, etc.
  • Product Mockups: One technical package + detailed render for a product, clothing item etc.

Photo Production

  • Base Shoot: 100 edited photos with color grading, etc. (one hour minimum)
  • Additional Edits: For each additional set of 50 edited photos
  • Optional Styling: Hair, make-up and outfit curation, shopping, and execution

Video Production

  • Base Shoot: One final video edit with color, transitions, etc. + b-roll footage (one hour minimum)
  • Additional Edits: For each additional video edit with color, transitions, etc.
  • Optional Styling: Hair, make-up and outfit curation, shopping, and execution

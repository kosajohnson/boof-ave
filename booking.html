Services and Booking

Our services are offered with clearly defined pricing, deliverables, formats, and revision limits to ensure a focused and intentional creative process. Extra edits, revisions, or project expansions may be purchased in addition to any service, and all hourly services carry a one (1) hour minimum. All services come with a complementary 30-minute introductory session to discuss service expectations and clarify client needs. After submitting the booking form, you’ll receive an email from boofavemgmt@gmail.com to begin the consultation process and set your project in motion.
